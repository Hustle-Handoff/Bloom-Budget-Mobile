How to Use Bloom Budget
Mobile Version · Hustle&Handoff
1
Log a Transaction

Tap Log Transaction in the top nav. Pick a date, choose a category, enter the amount, and add an optional note. Tap ＋ Add Transaction.

💡 Your dashboard updates automatically after each entry.

2
Set a Monthly Budget

Tap Set Budget and enter a spending target for each category. These appear as progress bars on your dashboard so you can see how close you are.

💡 Budget amounts stay saved until you change them.

3
Use the Bill Calendar

Tap 📅 Calendar to see bills on a monthly grid. Bills you log appear automatically. Tap any day to add a custom note or reminder.

💡 Use Prev / Next to navigate months. Today is highlighted in pink.

4
Sync with Desktop
📱 Mobile → Desktop: Export & Data → ⬇ Export Everything → transfer CSV to your computer → open desktop version → Export & Data → 📥 Import CSV.
💻 Desktop → Mobile: Export from desktop → email/AirDrop/Drive the CSV to your phone → open this app → Export & Data → 📥 Import CSV.

💡 Transactions merge with no duplicates. Export regularly as a backup.

5
Back Up Your Data

Your data saves automatically in your browser, but clearing browser data will erase it. Export a backup CSV at least once a month via Export & Data → ⬇ Export Everything.

Quick Tips
💾 Auto-saves — every change saves instantly. No save button needed.
📅 Past months — use the month & year dropdowns in the header to review old months.
✈️ Works offline — after opening once, no internet connection needed.
🖨️ Print summary — tap Print Summary for a clean monthly overview to print or save as PDF.
